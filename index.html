Let's Collect Crystals!

Instructions

  1. The aim of the game is to get your crystals to equal the "Number To Get"
  2. Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your score. Each crystal has a different value that will change with the start of each new game.

    3. You win the game by matching your total score to the Number To Get.
    You lose the game if your total score goes above the Number To Get.
